Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a website?

Development time depends on the project complexity. A simple business website can be ready in 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects may take 1-2 months. We always discuss timelines individually with each client.

What services are included in the development cost?

The base price includes: website design, responsive layout, basic SEO optimization, analytics setup, testing, and launch. Additional services are discussed separately.

Do you provide technical support after launch?

Yes, we provide free technical support for 3 months after website launch. After this period, you can sign up for an ongoing maintenance contract.

Can the website design be changed after launch?

Yes, we can make design changes after launch. The scope and cost of changes are discussed individually based on the required modifications.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit card, or electronic payment systems. We work with both individuals and businesses.
